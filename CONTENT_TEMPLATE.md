## üìù Brand Voice & Tone

### Core Values
- **Professional & Trustworthy** - Medical expertise backed by science
- **Warm & Welcoming** - Approachable and non-intimidating  
- **Empowering** - Focus on client agency and self-care
- **Premium** - High-end, quality-focused language

### Writing Guidelines

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Use "you" and "your" to speak directly to clients
- Focus on benefits and results, not just features
- Use concrete examples and data when possible
- Keep sentences clear and concise
- Use active voice ("We transform..." vs "Transformations can be...")
- Include calls-to-action naturally
- Be authentic and conversational

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Use overly technical jargon
- Make exaggerated or unproven claims
- Write in first-person plural ("we" about clients)
- Use marketing clich√©s ("amazing," "incredible," "life-changing")
- Exceed 3 exclamation marks per page
- Leave readers confused about next steps

## ‚úèÔ∏è Editing Checklist

Before publishing any content, verify:

- [ ] Headline is specific and benefit-focused
- [ ] Opening paragraph hooks reader immediately
- [ ] No jargon without explanation
- [ ] One idea per paragraph
- [ ] Paragraphs are 2-4 sentences max
- [ ] Includes specific examples or data
- [ ] Tone matches brand voice
- [ ] Ends with clear next step (CTA)
- [ ] Proofread for typos and grammar
- [ ] Links verified and working
- [ ] Images have alt text
- [ ] Metadata complete (title, description)
